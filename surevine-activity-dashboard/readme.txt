== Description ==

Displays a realtime activity dashboard on your website. Pull in feeds from github, twitter, your blog & foursquares.

Features:
--------
 * Display an activity dashboard on your website
 * Pull in feeds from github, twitter, your blog.

Requirements/Restrictions:
-------------------------
 * Works with Wordpress 3.0+
 * PHP5
 * PHP register_globals and safe_mode should be set to "Off"

Credits:
-------------------------
* By Surevine (https://www.surevine.com)

== Installation ==

1. Install automatically through the `Plugins`, `Add New` menu in WordPress, or upload the `surevine-technical-dashboard` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.

2. Activate the plugin through the `Plugins` menu in WordPress. Look for the Settings link to configure the Options.

3. To display activity dashboard on your blog: add the shortcode `[surevine-technical-dashboard]` in a Page (not a Post). That page will become your activity dashboard. Here is how: Log into your blog admin dashboard. Click `Pages`, click `Add New`, add a title to your page, enter the shortcode `[surevine-technical-dashboard]` in the page, click `Publish`.
